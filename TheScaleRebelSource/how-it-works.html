Process

How it works

Four phases. Clear milestones. No mystery about what you're getting or when.

01

Discovery

You tell me what you need. I ask the hard questions — who's the site for, what should it do, what does success look like. We define scope and I give you a straight quote.

02

Design + Build

I design in the browser — no Figma hand-off delays. You see real progress on a real URL. Feedback is fast and changes happen in real time.

03

Launch

Tested across every device and browser. Domain connected, SSL configured, security hardened. Your site goes live and it's fast from day one.

04

Handover

You get everything: source code, documentation, hosting credentials. It's your site. If you never talk to me again, it still works perfectly.

What I Deliver

  • Complete source code — documented and portable
  • Hosting setup and deployment configuration
  • Responsive design across all screen sizes
  • SEO foundation, social metadata, and sitemap
  • Security architecture documentation

What I Need From You

  • Your brand assets — logo, colors, any existing imagery
  • Content or a rough outline of what each page should say
  • A few sites you like the feel of (optional but helpful)
  • Responsiveness — I move fast and I'll need your feedback
